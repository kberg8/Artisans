Programs

Individual Supported Employment (ISE)

Our agency offers a variety of services and support throughout the entire employmentprocess. Our staff works diligently to offer job placements in the community andprovides personalized support for each individual, centering efforts on the individual's interests and abilities.

Group Supported Employment (GSE)

Our Group Supported Employment services offer job positions in the community with continuous support provided by staff members. Individuals work in small groups and earn pay based on productivity. Staff members provide training, job development and the supervision necessary to create a constructive work environment.

Community Access

Participants enrolled in this program will receive services focused on activities and goals related to community engagement and integration.

Divison of Vocation Rehab (DVR)

Our Divison of Vocational Rehab offers services where we help individuals find a job in the industry to work independently without our support after obtaining a job.

About Artisans

More Information

The Artisans has been a proud partner of the Spokane Community since 1987. Our services continually transform to meet the needs of our clients. The Artisans began as an agency whose employment offerings centered around on-site woodwork.
