Suit Drive

This summer Artisans is partnering again with Men’s Wearhouse for the seventh annual National Suit Drive, the nation’s largest collection of gently used business attire for men. Together, they are encouraging residents in the Spokane community to donate their gently used business attire to help give a fresh start to disadvantaged workers still facing unemployment.

Along with more than 150 other nonprofit organizations across the country, The Artisans and National Suit Drive will distribute the items collected at the local Men’s Wearhouse locations. Items being collected include men’s and women’s suits, shirts, jackets, ties, belts, and shoes. Residents of Spokane may donate their gently used items now through June 30th at 5005 N. Division #105/106, Spokane, WA 99207.

About Artisans

More Information

The Artisans has been a proud partner of the Spokane Community since 1987. Our services continually transform to meet the needs of our clients. The Artisans began as an agency whose employment offerings centered around on-site woodwork.
